Dr. Piyush H. Desai

Dr. Piyush H. Desai is a senior Consultant Diabetologist and Endocrinologist with over 15 years of clinical experience in the comprehensive management of diabetes, obesity, thyroid disorders, hormonal imbalances, and metabolic diseases. His clinical practice focuses on evidence-based medicine, long-term disease control, and prevention of complications.

About Doctor

Dr. Piyush H. Desai is a highly respected Consultant Diabetologist and Endocrinologist with extensive clinical, academic, and research experience in diabetes and metabolic medicine. His patient-first philosophy emphasizes ethical practice, clear communication, and personalized treatment strategies tailored to individual metabolic needs.


An alumnus of Government Medical College, Surat, Dr. Desai holds an MBBS, MD in Internal Medicine, a Postgraduate Diploma in Endocrinology (UK), and advanced certifications in diabetology and obesity management. He is a Senior Fellow of the International Diabetes Federation (IDF) and a Fellow of the Royal College of Physicians (FRCP), UK.


Dr. Desai has special expertise in managing complex diabetes cases, gestational diabetes, obesity, thyroid disorders, PCOS, metabolic syndrome, and endocrine complications. He is actively involved in international clinical trials and research that shape modern diabetes and obesity treatment protocols.


In addition to clinical practice, Dr. Desai serves as Chief Endocrinologist at Desai Diabetes & Endocrine Clinic, Surat, and is an honorary endocrinologist at Government Medical College, Surat. His leadership roles in national and international diabetes organizations reflect his commitment to advancing standards of endocrine care.


As a clinician, researcher, and educator, Dr. Desai regularly contributes as faculty at national and international medical conferences and training programs. His approach to care emphasizes scientific rigor, ethical decision-making, lifestyle-focused intervention, and long-term patient empowerment.

Clinical Research & Academic Work

Dr. Desai has served as Principal Investigator and Co-Investigator in multiple international clinical trials involving GLP-1 receptor agonists, obesity therapies, and cardiovascular outcome studies. He has authored and co-authored several peer-reviewed publications and textbook chapters in diabetes and metabolic medicine.

Care Philosophy

Dr. Desai believes that successful diabetes and endocrine care depends on early diagnosis, patient education, lifestyle intervention, and sustained follow-up. His care philosophy combines scientific precision with empathy—ensuring patients feel informed, supported, and confident in managing their long-term health.
